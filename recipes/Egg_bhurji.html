Egg Bhurji Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • Chopped coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© onions until translucent.
  2. Add tomatoes, green chili, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook until soft.
  3. Crack eggs into the pan, add salt, and scramble them quickly.
  4. Cook until eggs are done to your liking.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot.
