Omelette Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional: chopped onions, tomatoes, cheese, herbs

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, add milk, salt, and pepper. Beat well.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture and let it cook for a few seconds.
  4. Use a spatula to gently lift the edges and let uncooked eggs flow underneath.
  5. Add optional fillings on one half of the omelette.
  6. Once the bottom is set and the top is slightly runny, fold the omelette in half.
  7. Cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate and serve hot.
