Orange Juice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 fresh oranges
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut the oranges in half and squeeze out the juice using a juicer or your hands.
  2. Strain the juice to remove seeds and pulp, if desired.
  3. Add sugar and stir well (optional).
  4. Serve chilled with ice cubes.
